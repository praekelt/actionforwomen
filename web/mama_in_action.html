Mama In Action

If you’re pregnant, just had a baby or want to
experience MAMA for yourself just follow
the simple steps below to get started.

Explore

Go to askmama.mobi on your internet­enabled cellphone and start getting relevant, personalised information based on your pregnancy and/or the age of your baby. Remember to register with your due date or your baby’s age, so that we can tailor information to you, each time you revisit the site.


Vodacom customers can access the MAMA mobi site via Vodafone Live, free of charge! On Vodafone Live, go to "More Menu's", "Health and Lifestyle" and then "MAMA", or use www.live.vodafone.com/mama


Mxit customers can access MAMA on Mxit, by going to: Discover Mxit ­ Apps & Games ­ Apps by Category ­ Health & Advice ­ MAMA. (Normal Mxit charges apply).

Learn

MAMA Quizzes is a great, interactive way to keep learning every step of the way. Dial *120*2112# to register for MAMA Quizzes, and then remember to dial back into the same line every "MAMA Monday" for your weekly quiz and tips from MAMA. The MAMA Quizzes are a personalised and convenient way to keep learning and exploring throughout your pregnancy, and during the first year of your baby’s life. (Quizzes cost 20c per 20 seconds)

Grow

The MAMA South Africa project is proud to be partnering with the National Department of Health in the roll­out of its maternal health sevice, MomConnect. MomConnect allows pregnant women to register into the official national pregnancy registry, and , once registered, to receive stage­based information on their cellphone. MomConnect also allows pregnant women and new mothers to engage further with the health system, by rating the service they receive at health facilities, by sending in a question, compliment or complaint, as well as by searching through Life Guides on their phones.


Pregnant and want more information about MomConnect?


Dial *134*550# and remember to go to your nearest government health facility to complete your registration for MomConnect. (Dialling this number is free from all networks in South Africa)
